Better Buy now wants to expand its offerings and intends to launch a new section of reward programs. Better Buy, earlier offering shopping products and services, is now introducing its own rewards program section. Consumers will now be able to earn and redeem reward points after every successful purchase. These points can be used for cash discounts or to buy new products.

Reward Tiers:

  • Monthly spend of ₹2000 – Bronze Badge with 5% cashback points on every purchase.
  • Monthly spend of ₹2000–₹5000 – Silver Badge with 7.5% cashback points plus free delivery on every purchase.
  • Monthly spend of ₹5000 or more – Gold Badge with 10% cashback points, free delivery, and exclusive monthly gifts.
